# Ground Branch

In this document, you will find the equipment regulations required to be part of the SPECTRE Task Force. These are not suggestions, while some of the items have some degree of personal preference (eg: positioning of items, positioning of sights on rifles, etc.) we strongly advise that all items should be present and in the correct amounts at all times. It is your responsibility to keep up to date with this document and to have the correct equipment when you join the server.

If a piece of equipment or clothing isn't shown in this document `it is prohibited`

**We highly suggest that you save these kits in-game.**
